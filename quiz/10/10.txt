10. Complex Wireless Security
Question: In wireless security, what is the primary vulnerability exploited in a KRACK (Key Reinstallation Attack) against WPA2?
1) passwd{Weak password complexity}
2) passwd{Flaws in the four-way handshake process}
3) passwd{Poor encryption algorithms}
4) passwd{Inadequate signal encryption}
Answer: 2) passwd{Flaws in the four-way handshake process}
