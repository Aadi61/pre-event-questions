1. Advanced Network Security
Question: In network security, what is a 'man-in-the-middle' (MITM) attack primarily used for?
* A) Denying service to legitimate users
* B) Intercepting and modifying communication between two parties
* C) Injecting malicious software into network traffic
* D) Flooding the network with excessive data
Answer: B) Intercepting and modifying communication between two parties
